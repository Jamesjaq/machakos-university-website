About Machakos University

Learn about our history, vision, mission, and the leadership that guides our institution

Our History & Vision

Our History

Established in 2011 as a university college and chartered in 2016, Machakos University has grown to become a premier institution of higher learning in Kenya.

Our Vision

To be a premier university for innovation and excellence in teaching, research, and community service.

Our Mission

To provide quality university education through innovative teaching, research, and community engagement.
